Movie Recommender Hybrid - Collaborative Filtering (Second Project)
Project Diary

This was my second major project. I started it because recommendation systems are everywhere in daily life: Netflix, YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, Amazon, and ecommerce platforms. I wanted to understand how they actually work.

I learned quickly that recommender systems are different from standard supervised or unsupervised ML. This project stands on its own with collaborative filtering and similarity models, so it felt like a new category of ML.

The hybrid system combines:
- user-based similarity
- item-based similarity
- content similarity from genres (TF-IDF)
- latent factors from SVD

I worked through cosine similarity, how to normalize ratings, and how to blend multiple signals to improve recommendations. I also learned to reason about latency and tradeoffs when combining multiple models.

Skills gained: collaborative filtering, cosine similarity, TF-IDF content features, SVD latent factors, hybrid weighting, and recommender system design.
