Image Classifier CNN - PyTorch Focus
Project Diary

This project felt totally different from my text-based work, and that is why I liked it. I first learned image classification in TensorFlow, but I switched to PyTorch because it felt more flexible and closer to research workflows.

I learned how to expand a small dataset with augmentation (rotation, zoom, and other transforms) and how to reduce overfitting with better training discipline. I also learned the difference between training a small custom CNN and using transfer learning (MobileNetV2).

Skills gained: image augmentation, overfitting control, transfer learning, PyTorch training loops, and evaluation workflow for CNN classifiers.
