Kyaw Htet Portfolio - App-Inspired Design
Project Diary

For v1, I researched many GitHub portfolio templates and still could not find one that matched my vision. I ended up using ChatGPT alone to scaffold and complete the first version.

Later, I rebranded this portfolio website in 2026 with the help of Codex. During the build I used Codex heavily. It felt easier than Antigravity and made multi-agent iteration much faster. I also studied many portfolio sites for reference and learned how to apply a unified layout system to an app-inspired grid.

Then, I focused on rebuilding my portfolio site end-to-end. I bought a domain from Namecheap and intentionally avoided the usual portfolio layout. I wanted an app-inspired experience that combines a CV, projects, and a blog in one place so everything is clear and easy to scan.

This is v2, an upgrade of my earlier portfolio. It took about 4-5 days to finish and helped me integrate all of my existing projects into a single page.

For app icons, I used Canva to keep visual quality consistent across projects.

Skills gained: portfolio product design, multi-agent development workflow (Codex), UI integration across projects, content curation, and visual consistency for app icons.
