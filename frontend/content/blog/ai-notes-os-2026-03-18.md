AI + Human Collaboration OS for Notes
Project Diary

I have had messy notes and a messy codebase since I started coding. Even when I organized them, they drifted again and again. To solve that, I designed an AI-native notes system with three layers: vault (curated knowledge), code (execution and projects), and storage (raw data and archives).

This structure keeps things stable over time because it separates truth, runtime, and heavy assets instead of mixing them together. It also makes it easier for AI and humans to collaborate without losing track of what is current, what is historical, and what is just raw source material.

Skills gained: systems thinking, information architecture, governance design, and long-term knowledge maintenance workflows.
